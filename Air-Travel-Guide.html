Overview

Format: How-to guide
Length: 39 pages
Audience: Inexperienced air passengers
Tools used: Microsoft Word and Adobe Photoshop
Last updated: May 1, 2020

About

This document was created to fulfill the requirements for Simon Fraser University's TCOM120: Technical Writing and Editing course.

In TCOM120, students were required to create a how-to guide and collaborate with an editing partner. The course covered topics such as plain language principles and technical editing techniques.

How to Plan and Prepare for Air Travel describes methods that help first-time flyers prepare for air travel procedures and experiences before, during, and after their flight. I have since updated the how-to guide to reflect recent changes in Canadian air passenger rights regulations.
