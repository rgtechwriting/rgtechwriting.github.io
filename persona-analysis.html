Hepatitis C Information Booklet:
Information Design & Persona Analysis

Overview

Format: Information design report and proof-of-concept
Length: 10 pages
Audience: Recently diagnosed hepatitis C patients 
Tools used: Microsoft Word, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign
Last updated: December 4, 2019

About

This document was created to fulfill the requirements for Simon Fraser University's TCOM210: Research, Analysis, and Information Design course.

In TCOM210, students were required to create an audience persona and design an information booklet intended for patients recently diagnosed with hepatitis C. The course covered topics such as subject matter expert interview techniques, audience and task analysis, and information design principles.

Hepatitis C Information Booklet: Information Design & Persona Analysis includes a persona profile for a 30-year-old patient with hepatitis C and a proof-of-concept for a hepatitis C information booklet.
